Artist Biography

Andrew Silva is a Las Vegas-based singer-songwriter crafting original music that draws from rock, blues, soul, and psychedelic rock — sounds rooted in the American past but built for right now.

His songs blend heartfelt storytelling with rich arrangements, earning comparisons to the Beatles, Pink Floyd, and Stevie Ray Vaughan. When he's not writing, he's on stage with the Andrew Silva Band, bringing those songs to life with a full live lineup built for the Las Vegas original music scene.
